Plain Cake Recipe



Description

Once you make a cake, you bake for the rest of your life. A cake is a basic skill every person should know. A plain cake is the best choice for a beginner and for a crowd. The cake can then be customised as per one's wish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Self Raising Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50 grams Butter (cooking oil will also do)
  • Caster Sugar (regular sugar also works)
  • Half cup Milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla food flavour (You can use another food flavour if you wish such as orange flavour)
  • Pinch of fine salt

Instructions

  1. Use a metal pan for the batter ideally. Whip the eggs and sugar and pinch of salt first. Use mixer or whisker. A fork also works. Make sure all sugar grains are dissolved.
  2. Preheat oven to 180C (350F). Meanwhile line a cake pan with foil or parchment or alternatively with a little oil. This ensures the cake does not stick and can come out easily.
  3. Sift in the baking powder and flour. Mix well.
  4. Heat the milk on low to medium fire. After two minutes add in the butter. When the butter has melted, add the milk and butter mixture to the batter.
  5. Once the batter is well mixed and gives a pleasant fragrance, add the vanilla extract. Mix in.
  6. Bake time! Pour in the batter into the cake baking pan. Bake for about 35 minutes. A fork poked in the centre of the cake should not have any wet batter on it, this is the sign that the cake is fully cooked.
  7. Serve warm or cold. Ideally slice the cake. (Optional, add icing and decorations).
